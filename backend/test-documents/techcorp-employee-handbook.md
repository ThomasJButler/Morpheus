## New Employee Onboarding

### Your First Week - Step by Step

**Day 1: Welcome & Setup**
1. Arrive at the office by 9:00 AM and check in at reception
2. Meet your onboarding buddy who will guide you through the day
3. Collect your laptop, badge, and welcome kit from IT (Room 204)
4. Complete mandatory security training (2 hours) via TechLearn platform
5. Set up your workstation with IT support
6. Attend the New Hire Welcome Lunch at 12:30 PM in the cafeteria
7. Complete benefits enrollment with HR by 4:00 PM

**Day 2: Systems & Tools**
1. Complete Slack workspace setup and join required channels (#general, #announcements, your team channel)
2. Set up your development environment following the Engineering Wiki
3. Configure two-factor authentication for all company accounts
4. Review the codebase introduction documentation
5. Meet with your manager for initial 1:1 (30 minutes)

**Day 3-4: Team Integration**
1. Shadow a team member on their daily work
2. Complete department-specific training modules
3. Get access to relevant repositories and project boards
4. Attend your first team standup meeting
5. Review current sprint goals and ongoing projects

**Day 5: Wrap-up**
1. Complete the onboarding checklist with your manager
2. Schedule recurring 1:1s for the coming weeks
3. Submit any remaining paperwork to HR
4. Provide feedback on your onboarding experience via the survey

### Required Training Completion

All new employees must complete the following within their first 30 days:
- Security Awareness Training (mandatory, 3 hours)
- Code of Conduct Review (mandatory, 1 hour)
- Diversity & Inclusion Workshop (mandatory, 2 hours)
- Product Overview Session (recommended, 1.5 hours)
- Engineering Practices Deep Dive (for engineers, 4 hours)

## IT & Security Policies

### Acceptable Use Policy

All TechCorp devices and network resources are for business purposes. Limited personal use is acceptable during breaks. The following are strictly prohibited:

1. Installing unauthorized software without IT approval
2. Sharing credentials or access tokens with anyone
3. Connecting to unsecured public WiFi without VPN
4. Storing company data on personal cloud accounts
5. Downloading pirated content or visiting malicious websites
6. Attempting to bypass security controls

### Security Requirements for Remote Work

Remote employees must adhere to the following security protocols:

**Physical Security:**
- Work in a private space where screens cannot be viewed by others
- Lock your computer when stepping away (Cmd+L or Win+L)
- Never leave your laptop in a visible location in vehicles
- Use privacy screens in public spaces

**Network Security:**
- Always connect through the company VPN (GlobalProtect)
- Never use public WiFi without VPN active
- Keep home router firmware updated
- Use WPA3 encryption on home networks (WPA2 minimum)

**Data Handling:**
- Never store sensitive data on local drives - use approved cloud storage only
- Encrypt all external drives with FileVault or BitLocker
- Delete sensitive files from Downloads folder after use
- Report any suspected data breach within 1 hour to security@techcorp.io

### Software & Tools

**Approved Collaboration Tools:**
- Communication: Slack (official), Zoom (video calls)
- Documentation: Notion, Google Docs
- Project Management: Jira, Linear
- Design: Figma
- Code: GitHub Enterprise

**Development Environment:**
- IDE: VS Code (recommended), JetBrains suite (licensed)
- Local databases: Docker containers only
- API testing: Postman, Insomnia
- CI/CD: GitHub Actions, ArgoCD

## Expense Reimbursement

### How to Submit an Expense Report

Follow these steps to submit expenses for reimbursement:

1. **Collect Documentation**: Keep all receipts - photo/scan within 24 hours
2. **Access Expensify**: Log in at expensify.techcorp.io using SSO
3. **Create New Report**: Click "New Report" and select expense category
4. **Add Expenses**:
   - Upload receipt image
   - Enter amount and date
   - Select expense category (Travel, Meals, Equipment, etc.)
   - Add business purpose description
5. **Submit for Approval**: Reports go to your direct manager
6. **Timeline**: Approved expenses are reimbursed within 5 business days

### Expense Categories & Limits

| Category | Per-Item Limit | Monthly Limit | Approval Required |
|----------|---------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Meals (client) | $100/person | $500 | Manager |
| Meals (team) | $50/person | $300 | Manager |
| Travel (flights) | $800 domestic | No limit | Manager + Finance |
| Travel (hotels) | $250/night | N/A | Manager |
| Equipment | $500 | $1,000 | Manager |
| Software | $200 | $500 | IT + Manager |
| Learning | $500 | $2,000/year | Manager |

### Pre-Approval Requirements

The following expenses require pre-approval BEFORE purchase:
- Any single expense over $1,000
- International travel
- Conference attendance
- Equipment for new hires
- Recurring subscriptions

Submit pre-approval requests via Expensify with business justification.

## Performance Reviews

### Review Cycle Timeline

TechCorp conducts performance reviews twice per year:

**Mid-Year Review (June)**
- Self-assessment due: May 15
- Peer feedback collection: May 15-31
- Manager review meetings: June 1-15
- Final ratings submitted: June 30

**Annual Review (December)**
- Self-assessment due: November 15
- Peer feedback collection: November 15-30
- Manager review meetings: December 1-15

- Final ratings submitted: December 31

| Rating | Description | Typical Raise Range |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|

## Policies & Compliance

### Code of Conduct Summary

TechCorp employees are expected to:
- Act with integrity in all business dealings
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect
- Protect confidential information and intellectual property
- Report concerns through appropriate channels
